About the project
The developer's problem

What happens when you reach your peak in a job? How can you even tell that you have? One day you may realize that you've implemnted all the features you've been assigned to, delivered all the updates, fixed all the bugs. Granted, more will come, they always do - small changes here and there, big changes once in a while, new technologies to include, vulnerabilities to resolve - just more of the same.

Most likely you reach this point after pursuing your career path for some time, maybe a decade or two. A journey that you've started so long ago you don't even know if you enjoy it anymore. You've achieved a lot, no doubt - learned so much, had so many experiences. People look up to you and you're confident in your abilities. But something is still missing.

It may time to put that wasted talend to use.

Detached Head was born out of the angst of choosing comfort over passion. There comes a moment when individuals get to really choose what they want to pursue. Not that it's never too late, but it may even be the perfect moment. We're dedicating ourselves to this adventure and I know we'll enjoy the ride.
